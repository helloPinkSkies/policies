# Pink Skies
## AI Transparency Policy

### How AI is used in Pink Skies
Pink Skies uses artificial intelligence (AI) to respond to what you write.

When you share a reflection, the AI generates a response based on your words. These responses are designed to help you pause, notice, and make sense of your own thinking.

The AI is there to sit alongside your thoughts, not to take them over.

### What the AI is (and isn’t)
The AI is not a person, and it doesn’t truly understand you or your experiences.

It doesn’t think, feel, or know things in the way a human does. Instead, it generates responses based on patterns in language.

Because of this, its responses can sometimes feel insightful — and at other times, they may miss the mark.

### How the AI is designed to behave
The AI in Pink Skies is designed differently from tools that aim to advise, optimise, or “fix” you.

It is designed to:
- Reflect on what you’ve written 
- Gently explore your perspective 
- Help you notice what might already be present in your thinking 
- Support your own clarity, rather than replace it

It is not designed to:
- Tell you what to do 
- Provide answers or solutions 
- Judge, diagnose, or evaluate you 
- You remain the person making sense of your own experience.

### Important limitations
The AI is not always accurate and may misinterpret what you write.

Its responses should be taken as prompts for reflection, not as facts, instructions, or conclusions.

Pink Skies does not provide professional advice of any kind, including medical, mental health, legal, or financial advice.

You should not rely on the AI as a substitute for professional support.

### Support and real-world help
Pink Skies is not a crisis or support service.

If you are experiencing distress, feeling overwhelmed, or need help, it’s important to speak to a qualified professional or a trusted person in your life.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, please contact your local emergency services.

### How your data is used with AI
When you submit a reflection, your input is securely processed by a third-party AI provider to generate a response.

We configure our systems to minimise data retention and do not use your reflections to train AI models.

Your data is handled in line with our Privacy Policy.

### Your role
Pink Skies is a tool to support your thinking, not replace it.

You are always in control of how you interpret and use what you see.
