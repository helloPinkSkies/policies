# Pink Skies
## Privacy Policy
Last updated: 03/05/2026

### Who we are
Pink Skies is operated by Georgia Carr and Harry Carr, who act as the data controllers for the purposes of UK data protection law.
We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) under registration number [XXXXXXX]. 
If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us at: privacy@getpinkskies.com 

### What Pink Skies does
Pink Skies is an app that allows users to write and reflect on their thoughts and experiences. It uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate reflective responses and summaries based on what users choose to share.

### The data we collect
We collect the following types of personal data:
- Information you provide
- Name 
- Email address 
- Age 
- Journal entries and reflections you choose to write 
- Saved insights and summaries 
- Marketing preferences 
- Journal entries may include sensitive personal information (such as health or personal experiences). This data is only processed when you choose to provide it.
- Account and technical data
- Login credentials (securely hashed) 
- Country 
- Device and usage data (via analytics tools such as Firebase) 
- Crash reports and diagnostics 
- Local device data
- Draft journal entries stored locally on your device (not sent to our servers unless submitted) 

### How we use your data
We use your data to:
- Provide and operate the app 
- Generate AI-based reflections and summaries 
- Maintain your account and history 
- Improve app performance, stability, and user experience 
- Respond to support or data requests 
- Send updates and communications where you have opted in 
- We only collect and process the data necessary to provide the service.
- We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising purposes.
- We may send service-related emails regardless of marketing preferences.

### Legal basis for processing
We process your personal data under the following legal bases:
- Contract – to provide and operate the Pink Skies app 
- Legitimate interests – to improve the app, maintain performance, and ensure security, where this is not overridden by your rights and interests 
- Consent – where required, such as for marketing communications 

- You can withdraw your consent for marketing at any time through your app settings or by contacting us.

### How AI is used
When you submit a journal entry:
- Your content is sent securely to a third-party AI provider (OpenAI) for processing 
- The AI generates a response based on your input 
- This response is returned to you within the app 
- This processing may take place on servers located outside the UK, including the United States.
- We configure our AI provider to minimise data retention and do not use your journal content to train our own models or allow it to be used to train third-party models.
AI-generated responses:
  - May not always be accurate 
  - May misinterpret your input 
  - Are not professional advice 
  - Should not be relied on as a substitute for professional or medical support 

### Sharing your data
We only share your data where necessary to operate the app, including:
- OpenAI (for AI processing) 
- Firebase (Google) (for analytics and crash reporting) 
- AWS / Supabase (for secure data storage and infrastructure) 
- Where required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety
- We do not sell your personal data.

### International data transfers
Some of the services we use (such as OpenAI) process data outside the UK.

Where this happens, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in line with UK data protection law, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

### Data storage and security
We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) 
- Encryption at rest 
- Role-based access controls 
- Secure cloud infrastructure 
- Access to user data is restricted and monitored.

### Data retention
Journal entries and related data are stored until you choose to delete them 
You can permanently delete your data at any time within the app by going to:
- Your Profile → Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete Data 
When you delete your data:
- It is permanently removed from active systems 
- This includes journal entries, summaries, and account data 
- Data may remain in secure, encrypted backups for a limited period (up to 7 days) before being permanently deleted.
- We do not actively process data held in backups.
- We may retain certain data where required or permitted by law.

### Your rights
You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you 
- Request a copy of your data 
- Correct inaccurate information 
- Request deletion of your data 
- You can delete your data at any time directly within the app, or by contacting us at: privacy@getpinkskies.com
- We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

### Age restrictions
Pink Skies is only available to users aged 18 and over.

We rely on self-declaration at sign-up and do not knowingly collect data from children.

### Safety and support
Pink Skies is not a mental health or crisis support service.

If you share content indicating distress, the app may provide general guidance or signpost to external support services. However, it cannot provide real-time or professional assistance.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, you should contact emergency services or a qualified professional.

### Data breaches
In the unlikely event of a data breach:
- We will assess the risk and take appropriate action 
- Where required, we will notify the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) within 72 hours 
- We will notify affected users where there is a high risk to their rights and freedoms 

### Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app or by other appropriate means.
