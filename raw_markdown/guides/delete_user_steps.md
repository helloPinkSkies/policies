# Pink Skies

## How to delete your user account

### In-App Steps

1. Browse to the "Your Profile" screen
2. Tap the "Settings" option
3. Tap the "Account" option
4. Tap the "Delete Account" option
5. Review the pop-up information. Remember your subscription to Pink Skies is handled through the App/Play Store.
6. Tap "Delete Account"

Your user account is now deleted. This action is unrecoverable.

### Out of App Steps

1. Browse to this page: www.getpinkskies.com/support
2. Log a support ticket, requesting your user account to be deleted.
3. A member of staff will contact you promptly and coordinate the deletion for you.
